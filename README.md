# Adventure-work
Sales and Profitability Analysis using Excel, SQL, and Tableau. Includes KPI calculations, data cleaning, and interactive dashboard.

## Tools Used:
- Power BI
- Excel
- SQL
- Tableau

## Project Description:
-Analyzed 60,000+ sales records from the Adventure Works dataset to evaluate revenue, production cost, and profitability trends.
-Performed data cleaning, validation, and transformation using Excel (Power Query, Pivot Tables, formulas).
-Calculated key KPIs including Total Sales, Total Cost, Profit, Profit Margin %, and % of Total Sales.
-Used SQL for data extraction, joins (FactInternetSales, DimProduct, DimCustomer, DimDate), and business query analysis.
-Built interactive Tableau dashboard with filters for Year, Region, and Product Category.
-Created visualizations such as Sales by Category, Profit by Region, and State-wise Sales Map.
-Added reference lines, KPI cards, and percentage analysis for management insights.
-Identified top-performing product categories and low-profit regions to support data-driven decisions.

## Key Insights:
Highest Sales in Q4 – The company generated maximum revenue during the fourth quarter, showing strong seasonal demand.
- Top Performing Region – North America contributed the highest sales compared to other regions.
- Best-Selling Products – Mountain Bikes and Road Bikes generated the majority of revenue.
- Customer Segment Insight – Customers aged 30–45 were the most active buyers.
- Profit Margin Analysis – Certain product categories showed high sales but lower profit margins, indicating pricing improvement opportunities.
- Online vs Offline Sales – Online sales showed steady growth over time.
